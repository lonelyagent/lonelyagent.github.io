Why HTML5 game portals still matter

Modern browser engines are fast enough for casual multiplayer and polished single-player loops. For players, that means zero install friction. For publishers, it means distribution through the open web instead of only app stores.

Good genres for browser play

  • Racing and endless runners
  • Sports skill games
  • Arcade and reflex games
  • Puzzle and idle games

Quick checklist

  • Loads in under a few seconds
  • Readable on phone screens
  • Keyboard + touch friendly
  • Clear restart loop
