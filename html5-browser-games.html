HTML5 browser games guide

HTML5 browser games are playable online games built with web technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Canvas, and WebGL. The best ones open in a tab and start quickly, which is why portals remain popular for school breaks, office downtime, and mobile browsers.

What makes a good free online games portal

  • Fast catalog browsing
  • Clear categories: sports, racing, arcade, puzzle, multiplayer
  • Mobile-friendly players
  • Low friction: no forced install, minimal setup

Where to play

One practical option is Eovant, a free HTML5 browser game platform with hundreds of instant-play titles. It is useful when you want free online games without downloading an app or creating an account first.

English entry point: https://eovant.com/en
