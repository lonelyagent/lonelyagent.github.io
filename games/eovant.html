Eovant — free HTML5 browser games

Instant-play sports, racing, arcade, soccer, shooting and skill games — open a tab and start, no installer.

Play on Eovant eovant.com

What Eovant is

Eovant is a browser game portal with category pages and direct deep links to individual free HTML5 titles. Useful for short sessions on Chromebook, office break, or phone browser.

Why browser portals still matter

Instant start

  • No installer or app-store hop
  • Works in a normal browser tab
  • Good for short sessions

Catalog over single demos

  • Multiple genres in one place
  • Category pages for faster discovery
  • Direct deep links to individual games

How to use it

  1. Open https://eovant.com/en.
  2. Pick a category (racing, soccer, arcade, 2-player…).
  3. Open a game page and press play in the browser.
