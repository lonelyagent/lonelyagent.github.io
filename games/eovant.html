Eovant — free HTML5 browser games

Eovant is a free HTML5 game platform focused on instant play: open a tab, pick a category, and start sports, racing, arcade, soccer, shooting or skill games without installing a launcher.

Play on Eovant → eovant.com English hub

What Eovant is

Eovant is a browser game portal, not a single embed. The English catalog currently lists dozens of free HTML5 titles across multiple categories, with direct game pages such as Basket Random, Drift Hunters and Subway Surfers.

That makes it useful for short sessions on a Chromebook, office break, or phone browser: no download, no account wall just to browse, and categories that match common casual-play moods.

Why browser portals still matter

Instant start

  • No installer or app-store hop
  • Works in a normal browser tab
  • Good for 5–15 minute sessions

Catalog over single demos

  • Multiple genres in one place
  • Category pages for faster discovery
  • Direct deep links to individual games

How to use it

  1. Open https://eovant.com/en.
  2. Pick a category (for example racing, soccer, arcade or 2-player).
  3. Open a game page and press play in the browser.
  4. Bookmark the English hub or a favorite category for next time.
