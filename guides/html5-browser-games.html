HTML5 browser games guide

How to evaluate free no-download game portals, and what to look for beyond a single embed demo.

Browser games vs installers

HTML5 browser games win when you want a short session without a launcher. The useful unit is often not one game file, but a portal with categories and deep links.

Checklist for a good free portal

  • Instant play in a normal browser tab
  • Readable categories (sports, racing, arcade, skill…)
  • Stable deep links to individual games
  • Mobile-friendly pages
  • A catalog large enough that you can switch titles quickly

Example: Eovant

Eovant is a free HTML5 browser game platform with category pages and dozens of instant-play titles. See the full project notes and sample list on the Eovant page.
