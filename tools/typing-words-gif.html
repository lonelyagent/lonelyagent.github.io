Typing Words GIF Generator

Create animated typing text GIFs online for free. Useful for GitHub README headers, Discord banners, loading screens, marketing mockups, and any place you want a typewriter-style text animation without recording your screen.

Open Typing Words GIF → typingwordsgif.com

What it is

Typing Words GIF is a free online typing text GIF maker. You type the words, tune the look, preview the animation, then download a real animated GIF. There is no signup wall and no watermark on the export.

The generator runs in the browser, so your text stays on your device while you design the animation. That makes it a practical “typing words gif” tool for people who just need a clean motion-text asset quickly.

Core features

Design controls

  • Fonts, size and colors for text and background
  • Typing speed and loop behavior
  • Cursor style for the typewriter effect
  • Transparent background option for dark or light layouts

Output

  • Download a real animated GIF
  • No watermark on free exports
  • Works for README, social, email and demos

How to make a typing text GIF

  1. Open typingwordsgif.com.
  2. Enter the words or short lines you want to animate.
  3. Pick font, size, colors and typing speed so the preview matches your brand or README style.
  4. Click generate and download the typing text GIF.
  5. Drop the file into GitHub, Discord, a landing page, or a presentation.
